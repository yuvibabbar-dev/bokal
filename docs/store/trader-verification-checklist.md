# Bokal — EU-DSA Trader Verification Checklist

> Source: `docs/business/2026-07-13-business-recommendations.md` §7. Required to remain
> distributed in the EU under the Digital Services Act. Complete in the Chrome Web Store
> developer dashboard.

**Important — publicly displayed:** the legal name, business address, and contact details
entered here are shown publicly on the store listing. Use a **business address or registered
agent address**, not a home/personal address.

- [ ] **Legal (trader) name** — the real, registered business name (not a personal name, not
  "Bokal").
- [ ] **Physical business address** — a business or registered-agent address that is safe to
  display publicly. Do not use a home address.
- [ ] **Email address** — a monitored contact email (can be the same as the privacy-policy
  contact, e.g. `yuvisbabbar@gmail.com`, or a dedicated business address if one exists).
- [ ] **Phone number (SMS-verifiable)** — a number that can receive the SMS verification code
  Google sends during trader verification.
- [ ] **DUNS number** — optional; only provide if one already exists, not required to publish.

Complete this before submitting for review — CWS blocks EU distribution until trader
verification is finished.
